About us.

Pneumothorax, also known as collapsed lung, is an abnormal collection of air in the space between the chest wall and the lung. Its symptoms include severe pain and shortness of breath. It can be a life-threatening condition that needs urgent attention. Its detection can be difficult, and a chest x-ray is commonly used. Our Pneumothorax Classifier takes in x-ray images and analyses them to detect whether or not Pneumothorax may be present to aid medical professionals with making a diagnosis.

Our model is built using TensorFlow Keras and was trained and tested on Microsoft's Azure cloud platform, specifically their Machine Learning studio. Our front-end uses HTML, CSS and JavaScript, along with the Bootstrap framework.
